FAQs

Why am I seeing a watermark?
Watermarks are removed instantly for Pro members or during the free trial.
How do I cancel my free trial?
Go to Subscription → Manage Subscription. Cancel anytime before the 7-day trial ends to avoid charges.
Why is my image not loading?
Make sure your image is under 10MB and you have a stable connection.
Can I restore purchases?
Yes, simply sign in with the same email you used before.
